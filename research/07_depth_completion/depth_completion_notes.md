# Depth Completion

## Problem
Sparse LiDAR (e.g. 64-line) + RGB image → dense depth map

## Key works
- PENet (2022): two-branch CNN
- Park et al. CVPR 2024: test-time adaptation for domain gap
- Park et al. CVPR 2024: flexible completion for very sparse (4-line)
- All-day (IROS 2024): SpaDe + uncertainty fusion for day & night

## Application
Autonomous driving: sensor fusion between LiDAR and camera
