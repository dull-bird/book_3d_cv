# 6D Pose Estimation

## FoundationPose (CVPR 2024 Highlight, NVIDIA)
- B Wen et al. 618 citations
- Unified framework: model-based (CAD) + model-free (reference images)
- Refine Net: CNN+Transformer, Δt+ΔR
- Score Net: hierarchical comparison + contrastive learning
- LLM-assisted texture augmentation
- BOP leaderboard #1

## Freeze (ECCV 2024, 68 citations)
- Training-free: geometric+vison foundation models
- No fine-tuning needed

## Pos3R (CVPR 2025, 9 citations)
- 6D pose for unseen objects, DUSt3R-like pointmap approach

## FreePose (2025)
- Zero-shot using pretrained foundation models
